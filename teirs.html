Sponsorship Tiers

🌿 Green Level Sponsor

Investment begins at: $100 per event hour

Perfect for businesses that want to support a specific community event while demonstrating their commitment to local families.

Includes:

  • Public recognition during the event:
    • “Today’s free balloons are made possible through the generosity of ABC Bank. Thank you for investing in our community!”
  • Company logo displayed on booth signage throughout the event.
  • Recognition in event-specific social media posts before and after the event.
  • Recognition as a Community Partner helping provide free balloon creations for local children.

🌼 Silver Partner

Investment begins at: $200 per event hour

Perfect for businesses that would like a meaningful presence at community events—even when their staff cannot attend.

We’ll professionally represent your business while creating memorable interactions with families.

Includes everything in the Green sponsorship, plus:

  • Poppy, our interactive mascot, will naturally thank and recognize your business throughout the event in age-appropriate, story-driven conversations.
  • Your logo featured on compatible digital displays, including animated signage, digital pins, holographic fans, and wearable displays (venue permitting).
  • Collaboration on a custom balloon design, giveaway, or themed promotion that reflects your business and the event.
  • Option for your company to provide brochures, promotional materials, coupons, or event information to be displayed and distributed from our booth.

🌻 Season Community Supporter

Annual Investment: $5,000

Become one of Meadow Muffin Menagerie’s featured Community Partners for the entire event season.

Rather than sponsoring a single event, your business becomes part of a season-long effort to create joyful experiences for families throughout the region.

Includes:

  • Featured logo placement on Meadow Muffin Menagerie signage throughout the season.
  • Recognition on our website as a Featured Community Partner.
  • Weekly social media recognition throughout the event season.
  • Ongoing recognition within Poppy’s interactive adventures in a natural, family-friendly way.
    • “Poppy’s Favorite Dentist”
    • “Poppy’s Community Bank”
    • “Poppy’s Adventure Partner”
    • “Poppy’s Healthy Smile Partner”
  • Includes one annual exclusive family-friendly event for your company (promotions, grand openings, customer appreciation events, community campaigns).
  • Recognition at participating festivals, farmers markets, fairs, libraries, and community events throughout the season.
  • Annual Community Impact Report highlighting event participation, photographs, social media reach, and the positive impact made possible through your partnership.

🌟 Poppy’s Patron (Exclusive)

Annual Investment: Custom Proposal

Reserved for one exclusive organization each season.

Our Presenting Community Partner becomes the title sponsor for Meadow Muffin Menagerie’s public community appearances.

Example:
“The Air Benders Community Tour, Presented by ABC Bank.”

Includes everything in the Season Partnership, plus:

  • Exclusive title sponsorship throughout the season.
  • Premium logo placement on our event trailer, team uniforms, banners, and major signage.
  • Featured recognition on our website and social media as our exclusive Presenting Partner.
  • Exclusive business category protection (only one bank, one hospital, one insurance agency, etc.).
  • Customized community engagement opportunities designed specifically around your organization’s goals.
  • Priority scheduling for employee appreciation events, customer events, and community celebrations.
  • First opportunity to renew sponsorship each season.
